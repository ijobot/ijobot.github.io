About Us


At HMJ Travel, we take our own passion for travel and channel it into crafting unique, memorable, and often life-changing adventures for our clients. Each member of our team has travelled extensively - they've seen the world and all the beauty it has to offer - and they want to share that with you. Drawing on the connections we've made with other travellers and with members of local communities, we know where to go, how to get you there, and all of the must-see destinations once you arrive.

Looking for an exotic adventure across Asia? We've got you covered. Want to walk the paths of history on a grand tour across Europe? We can help. Just need to relax? Just take a breath, and we'll take it from here.

We have plenty of affordable packages to chose from and a number of financing options, so the only thing you'll need to worry about having your passport on hand!


A Personal Touch


If you want that once in a lifetime trip to be extra special, we also offer tailored travel packages, which are designed with you in mind. We can pair you up with one of our travel experts, who will meet with you to discuss everything that you're looking to get out of your trip and create a bespoke travel itinerary just for you.

For businesses, we also offer team-building and wellness packages, for companies of any size. Whether you're looking test your teamwork with an adventurous outing, or recharge your batteries, we've got you covered.


Local Connections


Each member of our team is brimming with insatiable wanderlust and has travelled across the world, where they've seen so many beautiful sights and met wonderful people. The connections that we've made across the world, both with local communities and with fellow travellers, allow us find exactly what you're looking for on your trip. We can find the best restaurants with the most authentic food. We can find the quietest beaches and the best views. Whatever you're looking for, we can find.
